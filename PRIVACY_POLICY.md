# Privacy Policy for Mini Golf Tracker

**Effective Date:** May 11, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the **Mini Golf Tracker** app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices. 

## Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

*   **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. This is required to save your scores and progress.
*   **Gameplay Data:** We collect and store your mini golf scores, game history, and preferences to provide the core functionality of the Application.
*   **Usage Data:** The Application may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the user experience.

We use **Google Firebase** (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore) as our backend service provider to securely store and process this data. 

## Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?
This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

## Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?
We only share your information with third parties in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.
*   **Service Providers:** We use third-party services (such as Google Firebase) to facilitate our Service, to provide the Service on our behalf, or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf.
*   **As required by law:** We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process.

## Security
We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. All data is transmitted securely and stored using industry-standard encryption provided by Google Firebase.

## Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.
